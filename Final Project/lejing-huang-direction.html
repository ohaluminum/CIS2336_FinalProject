Building Map

Maps play a significant role in our daily life. Especially when people are new to an unfamiliar environment, maps are indispensable.

The University of Houston has a big campus with over 120 buildings alone. One of the problems that every new uh student face at the beginning of the semester is that they often get lost on the campus, unable to find the classroom for the upcoming class. Suppose a student wants to go to the college of Technology but doesn't know where the building is. This map first allows him to determine the general direction of his target building through the relative position of each reference building on the map. Secondly, the navigation function attached to the map can directly guide students to their destinations.

In addition to the most basic navigation function, the map also enables students to have a general understanding of the surrounding buildings of the College of Technology, so that students can get familiar with the campus environment more quickly.

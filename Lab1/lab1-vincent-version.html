My Goals

Let's start with my long-term goals. My first goal is not only to get the major of CIS but also to get the minor in computer science. After I took COSC1306, which is also a required class in CIS, I found that I was interested in learning programming. I believe this interest is enough to offset the boredom of programming itself and make me more motivated to study in this major.

My second goal is to graduate in four years. If I can take one or two courses during the summer vacation, I believe that I can graduate on time. My third goal comes from life. I hope I can keep exercising and become healthier.

I also have a small goal for this class. I hope that by the end of this course, I can also be proficient in using HTML and other languages to write web pages with a sense of design and creativity.
